Us The Humans – Abey Noble

The concept for the first design is simple: We’re all human. We’re all just human. I’m human. You’re human. We make mistakes. We’re creative, weird, and crazy. Sometimes we feel like failures and other times like success stories. We’re all human. And hopefully, by participating in this type of thinking, we take that step back and embrace that feeling in a world where it can be so easy to forget it.
